Setup Guide — Shopify (Dropshipping Track)

Your Store. Live Today.

This guide is for the Dropshipping track. If you're on Digital Products or Affiliate, you don't need Shopify yet — but your customers do, which is why it's in the affiliate tools.

Before you build — find your product first

Don't build the store until you know what you're selling. Most people set up a beautiful store and then try to find products. Wrong order. Product first. Store second.

Where to find products: TikTok Creative Center → Top Products. Look for items with lots of organic videos, high engagement, and no obvious big-brand competition.

Step by step
  • ✓
    1. Start your Shopify trialshopify.com → Start free trial → Use affiliate link from the Tools page to support Startiq
  • ✓
    2. Pick a clean themeThemes → Free themes → Dawn or Refresh. Don't spend money on a paid theme yet. Clean and fast beats fancy.
  • ✓
    3. Add your productProducts → Add product → Title, description (benefits not features), price, photos. Use supplier photos or create your own with Canva.
  • ✓
    4. Connect your supplierInstall DSers (free) from the Shopify App Store → connect AliExpress → find your product → import to store. Orders fulfill automatically.
  • ✓
    5. Set your pricingRule: price at 3x your product cost minimum. If product costs $8, sell at $24+. Always include shipping in your price — free shipping converts better.
  • ✓
    6. Set up paymentsSettings → Payments → Shopify Payments (or Stripe). Add PayPal as a second option — some customers only buy with PayPal.
  • ✓
    7. Add essential pagesCreate: Refund Policy, Privacy Policy, Shipping Policy. Shopify generates templates for all of these automatically in Settings → Policies.
  • ✓
    8. Test orderPlace a test order. Confirm DSers picks it up. Confirm the customer confirmation email looks right. Then go live.

FAQ

Questions They Will Ask.

Answer these before they ask. Use these in DMs, on your Stan.store page, and in your content.

"Is this a course?"

No. It's a system. A course gives you information. This gives you the exact steps, templates, scripts and tools to execute from day one. You don't watch it — you use it.

"I'm a complete beginner. Is this for me?"

Yes — that's exactly who it's built for. You don't need experience. You need a clear starting point and a system that tells you what to do next. That's this.

"How fast can I make money?"

That depends entirely on how fast you execute. Some people make their first sale within a week. Most take 2–4 weeks. Nobody who follows the system is still at zero after 30 days — unless they didn't use it.

"Do I need to spend more money after this?"

No upfront costs required. ManyChat is free up to 1000 contacts. Stan.store is free to start. Canva has a free plan. The only cost is time — and this system minimizes that too.

"What if it doesn't work for me?"

The system works. What sometimes doesn't work is the execution. If you go through it and genuinely get stuck, reply to your welcome email and I'll help you figure out where the block is.

"Which track should I choose?"

Open the "Pick Your Track" page in the sidebar. Three questions. Five minutes. Done.

"What's the difference between $29 and $79 Pro?"

The $29 system is complete — everything you need to start. The $79 Pro adds a 30-minute onboarding call with Alexandra where you go through your specific situation and build a plan together. If you want to move faster and avoid guesswork, Pro is worth it.

"Can I do this alongside a full-time job?"

Yes. That's how most people start. The system is built to run in the margins — 30–60 minutes a day is enough to get started. ManyChat automates the DMs so you don't have to be online all day.
